Becoming Review

Author:

Michelle Obama

Genre:

Memoir, biography

This is a good book. Michelle Obama and her struggles in being a mother and a First Lady were admirable and enjoyable to read. This book helped paint her in a light she wanted to be painted in, as opposed to the media protrayal. Personally, I am not someone interested in politics so it did not strike any chords with me.

TL;DR 3-stars, an interesting book (maybe a 4-star but the topic was not interestin to me)
