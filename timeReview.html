A Brief History of Time Review

Author:

Stephen Hawking

Genre:

Popular science

Whether you have any large background in physics or not, this book is packed with interesting content that makes you wonder why physics wasn't more fun in high school. His explanations were clear. It did not feel like reading a textbook as some might expect. Instead it invoked feelings of child-like curiosity.

TL;DR 4-stars, interesting and mind-opening
