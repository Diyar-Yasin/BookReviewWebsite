Complete Cthulu Mythos Tales Review

Author:

H.P. Lovecraft

Genre:

Horror fiction

It may not be fair to review a giant collection of short stories as one, but, HP Lovecraft' universe is steadily improved the more you read. This book is the best horror I have ever read. It never relies on jumpscares, instead it delivers a feeling of encroaching dread. At the end of every story there is always a realization at the last sentence to leave you thinking.

This book is a difficult read and is quite long (600 pages). However, it is phenomenal at what it tries to achieve. If you are unsure as to how words on a page can be scary, look no further.

TL;DR 5-stars, one of my top books
