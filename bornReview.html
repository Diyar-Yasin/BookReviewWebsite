Born A Crime Review

Author:

Trevor Noah

Genre:

Autobiography, Humour

A book that not only made me laugh, but informed me about the world. Prior to reading this book, I had only ever heard the word appartheid. I learned, through the rich experiences of Trevor Noah, what appartheid was like as well as the unique predicament those of mixed black and white hertiage had.

The characters were likable and the store was heartfelt. This is an easier read as well so I would recommend it to anyone.

TL;DR 4-stars, a great book
