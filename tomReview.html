Uncle Tom's Cabin Review

Author:

Harriet Beecher Stowe

Genre:

Like all older classics, this one started off slow. Older English and southern-accented text made it tough to get through the first half of the book if I am being honest. The payoff, however, was worth it. I did not expect to feel so much for the main character. His unbreakable will was awe-inspiring. I might have teared up a bit.

TL;DR 4-stars, a heartbreakingly tragic story that is worthy of being a classic.
