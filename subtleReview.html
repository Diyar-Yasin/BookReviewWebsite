The Subtle Art Of Not Giving A F*ck Review

Author:

Mark Manson

Genre:

Self-help

This book had interesting ways to outlook on life, however, the novelty of using curse words in a book wears thin after the millionth time. Also, Mark writes to the reader as if we have never read a book before in our lives, to a condescending extent.

TL;DR 2-stars, meaning extracted as a self-help book is, but boring to read.
